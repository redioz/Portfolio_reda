Mini Jacuzzi Project

From September 2022 to June 2023, I managed the development of a mini jacuzzi, which included the design of the frame, pumping, and heating systems. This role involved applying DMAIC and FMEA methodologies, Kaizen principles like SMED, Kanban, 5S, Poka-Yoke, and Jidoka, and using advanced process mapping techniques to enhance operational efficiency and reduce maintenance costs.

  • Applied DMAIC and FMEA methodologies for continuous improvement.
  • Employed Kaizen principles to streamline production processes.
  • Developed comprehensive process maps to identify and eliminate waste.
